From Our Kitchen

Dishes Crafted with Heritage

Every recipe carries generations of knowledge — slow-cooked, spiced with care, and served with pride.

Kacchi Biryani

Our signature slow-cooked kacchi biryani — whole spices, marinated meat, and fragrant basmati rice sealed and cooked together for that unmistakable depth of flavour.

Mutton Rezala

A Bengal classic — tender mutton in a silky white gravy of yoghurt, nut paste, and gentle aromatics. A dish that tells the story of Dhaka's culinary heritage.

Mishti Doi & Sweets

No dawat is complete without the sweets. Creamy mishti doi, gulab jamun, and seasonal Bengali mithai — the perfect close to a remarkable meal.
