Weddings

Weddings & Shaadis

A shaadi is the grandest of occasions — a celebration of two families becoming one. We understand the significance of the dawat, the tradition of feeding guests with generosity and love, and we bring that spirit to every plate we serve.

Our wedding catering captures the full richness of Bengali cuisine: layered biryanis, slow-cooked kormas, fragrant curries, freshly made breads and desserts that guests will talk about long after the day is done. We handle setup, service and teardown — so your family can celebrate, not coordinate.

Typical range: 50–500 guests

What's Included

  • Curated starters selection — samosas, kebabs, tikkas, chutneys
  • Main courses — Kacchi Biryani, kormas, bhunas, seasonal curries
  • Full rice and bread selection including naan, paratha and jeera rice
  • Traditional desserts — Gulab Jamun, Ras Malai, Mishti Doi, Firni
  • Experienced serving staff in formal attire
  • Full crockery, chafing dishes and buffet setup
  • Setup before the event and teardown after

Funeral Catering

Funeral & Fatiha Catering

Providing a meal for mourners is a deeply rooted tradition in our community — a quiet act of love and solidarity. We approach this service with the utmost sensitivity, discretion and respect.

Our funeral and Fatiha catering is intentionally understated. Simple, wholesome food prepared with care. No fuss, no unnecessary ceremony — simply nourishment for those gathering to remember a loved one.

Typical range: 20–100 guests

What's Provided

  • Simple, respectful menu — plain rice, daal, korma, bread
  • Wholesome, home-style preparation
  • Quiet, unobtrusive service throughout
  • Respectful staff who understand the occasion
  • Flexible arrangements around ceremony timings
  • Delivery and setup at home or venue
