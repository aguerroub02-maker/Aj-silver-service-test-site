Our Story

From family kitchen to your celebration

Growing up in a Bengali household in Britain, food was never just sustenance — it was language. It was the slow simmer of dal on a Sunday afternoon, the scent of whole spices crackling in oil, the gathering of aunties in the kitchen arguing lovingly over whether the biryani needed more ghee. Every meal told a story, and every gathering was an occasion worth honouring. Those memories are the foundation of everything we do at AJ Silver Service.

As we grew older, we noticed a gap — a real, felt absence. The community deserved catering that reflected who we truly are: our flavours, our traditions, our warmth. Too many events were served food that felt generic, disconnected from the heritage and care that our celebrations deserve. We knew we could do better. Not just better food, but a better experience — one that felt like home, no matter how large the venue.

AJ Silver Service was born from that conviction. What started with small family gatherings — dawats for thirty, Eid lunches for the neighbours — grew steadily, event by event, family by family, into the full-service catering company you see today. We have now had the honour of serving hundreds of events across the UK, from intimate funerals to five-hundred-guest shaadis. Through it all, the food has never changed: it is made with the same hands, the same recipes, and the same love as it always was.

Our Values

Why Families Trust Us

Four pillars that sit at the heart of every event we cater — from the smallest gathering to the grandest celebration.

Authenticity

Every recipe carries the memory of a home kitchen. We use traditional Bengali techniques and whole spices — no shortcuts, no compromise. What you taste is what we grew up eating.

Halal

100% Halal sourcing from certified suppliers, every time. There is no grey area here. Your guests eat with full confidence, knowing every ingredient meets the highest standards.

Experience

Over a decade of events, from intimate family gatherings to 500-guest shaadis. We have seen and solved every challenge — and we bring that calm, practiced expertise to your celebration.

Community

Bengali-owned, community-minded. We understand your celebration because it is our celebration too. We speak your language — in every sense — and it shows in every dish we serve.

Trust & Standards

Community, Trust & Certification

We are a business built on trust — the trust of families who have welcomed us into some of their most significant moments. That responsibility is one we carry with deep sincerity. From the moment you enquire to the final dish served, our commitment is to your peace of mind.

Our Halal certification is not a marketing claim — it is a promise maintained at every step of our supply chain. Every supplier we work with is independently Halal-certified, and we review those relationships regularly. We are active members of the communities we serve, and our reputation within them is something we guard fiercely.

Whether you are organising a wedding for three hundred guests or a quiet Fatiha meal for close family, you can rely on us to arrive prepared, serve with care, and leave your venue as we found it. We have built our business entirely through word of mouth — and we intend to keep it that way.
